Overview

Purpose

Fashion, we all are part of that world. We do not buy clothing only for pleasure, in reality, it is a need. But, should we consider more than trends when shopping for new pieces to add to our closet? How can we be fashionable while avoiding overconsumption of fast fashion? Well, the answer is classic and ageless clothing that is less likely to end up in a landfill. Timeless Print curates for the comfortable and stylish staple pieces meant to make you feel effortlessly beautiful. Allow us to upgrade your closet with unique, stunning, and sustainable apparel that never go out of style and you will always love.

Audience

Timeless Print is a clothing boutique dedicated to provide women of all sizes with thoughtful apparel meant to last and be treasured for ages. We hand-select our garments for the ladies who love classic, romantic, feminine, and modest designs. Our goal is to add sustainability to fashion with effortless and always trending styles that make you feel comfortable every time you put them on. And our hope is to help our customers reflect their love for clothing and nature through their wardrobe.

Content

Who we are and what we do!

It can be challenging nowadays to find quality clothing that is fashionable and also relevant after one season. Fast fashion and “here today gone tomorrow” trends continue to become a norm in the industry, which inevitably hurts our planet by creating extra waste. Timeless prints deify these trends by offering sustainable fashion that will work for the consumer at any stage of their life, and that’s durable enough to pass it on. This site caters to a specific fashion style; minimal, modest, esthetically pleasing, and feminine. Our goal is to become the primary source of one of kind fashion for women of all ages and from all backgrounds who are looking for that outfit that will magnify their beauty and can be worn at a dinner party tomorrow or a night at home ten years from now. This site is a boutique of hand-picked designs that are “timeless” in nature and will always be in style. We are committed to having both, our customer and our planet, in mind with every fabric and design selection.

Images for the Home page

Our timeless styles

The items in our collection reflect a minimal and color neutral fashion style that is universally beloved. But don’t worry, we promise to have styles that will work for all body frames. We have all customers in mind so our collection is available in different sizes, ranging from XS to XXL. To ensure our customer’s satisfaction and trust, we have added the fabric content and approximate measurements for every piece available in our collection. Every piece in our site is hand-picked for its romantic, durable, and lasting timeless look. We house these pieces and sale them at attractive prices to help the classy and thoughtful ladies of the world refine their closet with pieces that will last you awhile. We help those looking to escape fast fashion by adding to their wardrobe one or many pieces that transcend the moment. Trust us, and we will refresh your image with apparel that will never lose its edge and is always inspired in nature. Between the online gallery, caring customer interactions, and an easy secure purchasing platform, our elegant lady customers will be able to follow lasting fashion, and identify the styles that look good on them tomorrow and in years to come. Being able to browse our gallery the customer will see just how the different clothing lines, styles and combinations go together and standout in a variety of everyday scenarios. If they are trying to revamp their work outfits, we can make them glow at the business meeting. If they are trying to standout in the room for their special evening, we can help them find the outfits that will continue to allow them to standout every time they wear it.

Let us hear from you

The website will help us show the customer the special selections of clothing that we offer to help them refine their wardrobe, in a more cost-effective manner. This requires more than just images of different pieces and combinations of clothing. It requires thoughtful interactions. Through our contact us page we will be able to achieve the personable relationship that we are seeking with the customers. By welcoming their suggestions, we can improve our services but more importantly, we can help them find the perfect garment that they have been looking for. Having a more direct communication with our customers and listening to their input can help us source the kind of pieces that meet their tastes, needs, and wants. This is our promise, that all of our customers will leave the site feeling good about their shopping habits and happier about what they will wear tomorrow.
